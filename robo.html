#6.Robotics


Description:A robot competition is an event where the abilities and characteristics of robots may be tested and assessed. Usually they have to beat other robots in order to become the best one. Many competitions are for schools but several competitions with professional and hobbyist participants are also arising.

  • Robotics - An Introduction
  • Accelerometer - Concepts & Applications
  • Electronics
    • Microcontroller - Architecture
    • Arduino Board - Design & Components

  • Programming - C++ Arduino Programming, IDE & Flashing
